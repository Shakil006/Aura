Labor Phases Guide
Early: 30–45 sec, every 5–20 min. Stay home, rest, call OB.
Active: 45–60 sec, every 3–5 min. Go to East Georgia Regional!
Transition: 60–90 sec, every 2–3 min. Almost there! 🌸

🚨 Go to ER Immediately If:

⚡ Heavy vaginal bleeding or large clots
⚡ Severe abdominal or pelvic pain
⚡ Severe headache + vision changes or face swelling
⚡ No fetal movement after 28 weeks
⚡ Fluid gushing from vagina (water breaking)
⚡ High fever over 100.4°F / 38°C
⚡ Contractions 5 min apart for 1 hour

📿 Daily Dhikr
سُبْحَانَ
SubhanAllah · سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ
Glory be to Allah · 33× after each prayer
✓
Recite SubhanAllah 33 times after Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. This is from the Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ.
الْحَمْدُ
Alhamdulillah · الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
All praise to Allah · 33× after each prayer
✓
Recite Alhamdulillah 33 times after every prayer. A simple word that fills the scales of good deeds.
أَكْبَرُ
Allahu Akbar · اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ
Allah is Greatest · 34× after each prayer
✓
Recite Allahu Akbar 34 times after every prayer. Together with SubhanAllah and Alhamdulillah, this completes the Tasbeeh of Fatima (RA).
☀️
Morning Adhkar
Ayatul Kursi + 3 Quls · After Fajr
✓
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ
Recite Ayatul Kursi (2:255), then Surah Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, and An-Nas each 3× after Fajr. This provides protection throughout the day.

3 Quls + Ayatul Kursi · After Asr/Maghrib
✓
Recite Surah Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, and An-Nas each 3× and Ayatul Kursi after Maghrib. The Prophet ﷺ said these protect from harm until morning.
💛

"Allahumma salli 'ala Muhammadin wa 'ala ali Muhammad"
Whoever sends Salawat upon the Prophet ﷺ once, Allah sends ten blessings upon them (Muslim). Especially powerful during pregnancy.
📖 Weekly Practices
📖
Surah Maryam
Weekly recitation · Blessed for pregnancy
✓
Surah Maryam tells the story of Maryam (AS) and the miraculous birth of Isa (AS). Traditionally recited during pregnancy for blessings on mother and child. Try to complete it once per week.
📖
Surah Yusuf
Weekly recitation · Beautiful character
✓
Surah Yusuf is recommended during pregnancy. It is said to be associated with beautiful character and grace for the child, by the permission of Allah. Read once per week.
🌴
Eat Dates Daily
Sunnah food · Rich in iron & energy
✓
Sunnah food — rich in iron, potassium, fiber and natural sugars. Research suggests dates may ease labor. Aim for 6–7 dates per day especially in T3. The Prophet ﷺ broke his fast with dates.
🕌
5 Daily Prayers
Pray sitting if standing is difficult
✓
If standing is difficult due to dizziness or discomfort, praying while sitting is perfectly valid. Never miss your connection with Allah. Salah is the pillar — it calms the heart during pregnancy.
🎵
Quran for Baby
From ~18 weeks · Baby can hear!
✓
From around 18 weeks, baby can hear sounds. Playing or reciting Quran aloud — especially Al-Fatihah, Ayatul Kursi, and the last three Surahs — is a beautiful practice for baby's spiritual development.
🤲
Dua Together (Iffat & Shakil)
Parents' dua for children is most powerful
✓
Making dua together as husband and wife for the baby is a beautiful act of worship. The dua of parents for their children is among the most powerful — never rejected. Do this together daily, especially before sleep.
